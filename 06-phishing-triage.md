# 06 - Phishing Triage

## Purpose

This section reviews a sample suspicious email in the fictional learning environment.

## Triage Checklist

- Is the sender address suspicious?
- Does the email create urgency?
- Are there unusual links?
- Are there attachment risks?
- Is the message asking for credentials?
- Does the domain look legitimate?
- Should the email be reported or blocked?

## Example Triage Table

| Indicator | Observation | Risk Level | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Urgent language | Email asks for immediate action | Medium | Treat with caution |
| Suspicious link | Link does not match the expected domain | High | Do not click |
| Credential request | Email asks for login details | High | Report as phishing |

## Student Note

This is a student-level phishing triage example, not a full forensic email analysis.

The goal is to practice checking common phishing indicators in a simple and structured way.

## Outcome

Suspicious emails should be reviewed using a clear checklist before any action is taken.
